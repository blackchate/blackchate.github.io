McGill Battlebots Club

Founded in 2019, The McGill BattleBots Club was created to provide practical learning opportunities for passionate students through a series of fun and creative battle bot competitions.

Aiming to boost mechatronics learning through hands-on projects and our equipment-lending system, the McGill Battlebots Club also provides discounted electronics, blueprints, code snippets, allowing empassioned students to easily take their creations from start to finish.
