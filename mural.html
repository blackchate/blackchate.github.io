Where Is The Mural? - NSBE x Google x HoodStock

Test your geographical knowledge of the city by pinpointing the Mural on the map. Mural data was obtained from the Montreal murals geojson and csv dataset (https://donnees.montreal.ca/). The Android Application was created using Android Studio, Google Maps API and Firebase.
