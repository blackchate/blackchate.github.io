Where Is The Mural?

A fun and interactive Geolocation Android app that lets users guess the location of murals in Montreal by providing the coordinates in the map.

NSBE x Google x HoodStock Hackathon Winner

Test your geographical knowledge of the city by pinpointing the Mural on the map. Mural data was obtained from the Montreal murals geojson and csv dataset (https://donnees.montreal.ca/). The Android Application was created using Android Studio, the Google Maps API and Firebase.
